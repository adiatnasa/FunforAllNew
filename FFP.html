Fact 1

Wombat poop is cube-shaped.
This unique shape prevents the poop from rolling away and helps wombats mark their territory.

Fact 2

Water can boil and freeze at the same time.
This phenomenon, called the triple point, occurs when temperature and pressure are just right for the three phases (gas, liquid, and solid) to coexist.

Fact 3

The Eiffel Tower can grow taller in the summer.
Due to heat expansion, the metal structure can stretch by up to 6 inches (15 cm) in warm weather.

Fact 4

A cloud can weigh more than a million pounds.
Despite their fluffy appearance, clouds are made of condensed water droplets, which add up to an enormous weight.

Fact 5

Octopuses have three hearts!
Two pump blood to the gills, while one pumps it to the rest of the body.
